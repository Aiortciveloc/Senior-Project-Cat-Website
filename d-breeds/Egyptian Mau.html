Egyptian Mau

The Egyptian Mau originates from North America and Europe, despite popular claims that it derives from Egypt. The Egyptian Maus are one of the only cats to be naturally spotted, and have smooth, glossy fur. Their coat can be silver, bronze, smoke, and black or shades and mixes of multiple of the colors. Egyptian Maus have unique hind legs, which makes them the fastest domesticated breed of cats out of them all. They have been reported to run at speeds up to 30 mph! Egyptian Maus like to spend time with humans, but like to do it on their own terms. Rarely will you see an Egyptian Mau let you command when you're going to pet it and where. If you follow through this breeds commands, they will be there for you when you're sleeping and sitting down watching television. Friendly to everyone in the family, they tend to be cautious with strangers and select their “Special Person,” which they will spend the most time with. Egyptian Maus are great for you if you're living alone and do not go out much.
