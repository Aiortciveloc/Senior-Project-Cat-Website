Asian Semi-longhair

The Asian Semi-longhair is a relatively new breed of cat; being developed back in the 1980's in the United Kingdom. This breed is very similar to the Asian Shorthair, except is has semi-long fur instead. The Asian Semi-longhair has a variety of coat and eye colors, and is noticed because of it's longer hair. They have flatter faces than most other cats. Curious and playful is what the Asian Semi-longhair is known for. They are always coming up with ways to pesture you for some love and attention. Asian Semi-longhairs love their owners very much if they treat them well, but do not like other cats too much. They can get jealous quite easily if you're caught seen with another cat. They are an excellent one of cat for a family.
