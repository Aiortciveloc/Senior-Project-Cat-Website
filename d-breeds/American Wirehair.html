American Wirehair

The American Wirehair breed originates from America. American Wirehairs have a unique coat that differentiates them from other breeds of cats. Their coat and whiskers start to limp and crimp to farm a "wire" look. The wirehair has high cheekbones and golden eye color. If this breed has any kittens, the litter they produce will surely be wire hair kittens too, as the wirehair trait is dominant. Additionally, Wirehairs are very resistant to disease, therefore you won't have to worry about taking them to the vet often! Personalities of this breed have been known as quiet and reserved, but very loving once they warm up to you. Once warmed up to their owners, they become very playful and will love to fall asleep curled up in your lap. They make a great overall breed type for family.
