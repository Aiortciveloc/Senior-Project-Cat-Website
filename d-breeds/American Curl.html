American Curl

This cat is recognized for its distinctively curl in the shape of the ears, as its name says. They have sweet expressions, and either have long or short silky coat and can be found in any color and range from 5 to 10 pounds, which is relatively medium-sized for a cat. The American Curl is a result of a natural genetic mutation that first appeared in Shulamith, a black kitten with ears curled backward making all american curls descended from shulamith. Personality wise, the american curl is a people-loving, active, curious, and smart cat. American Curls are great for family time because they stay active throughout all their years, and never die down in energy compared to most cat breeds.
