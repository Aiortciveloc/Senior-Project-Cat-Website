American Shorthair

The American Shorthair is the seventh most popular cat breed in the United States. These wonderful cats have a round face and shorter than average ears. Their pelt can range in 80 different colors, and produce many patterns, which make them one of the more beautiful cat breeds. American Shorthairs are known for their loveable personality and the fact that they commune well with kids and dogs, which make them an exceptional house pet. They take almost little to no maintence at all, besides cleaning the kitty litter, and essentially fend for theirselves. The American Shorthair originates from Europe (It is said that they kept American Shorthairs on the Mayflower to catch rats!) and were selectively bred to keep the good looks and the all american feel to the cat.
