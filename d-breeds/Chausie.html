Chausie

The Chausie originates from Northern Africa as wild cats. Chausie were purely wild until bred with domestic cat to transition them into a pet. This makes them fairly unique, as there isn't many other breeds of cat bred from an actual wild cat. The breed is fairly new, and was not spreading around the world until 1990, where it has been deported to the Americas and Europe. They are a shorthaired cat with an elegant coat. Chausie's coat can come in one of the three colors: solid black, black grizzled tabby, and brown ticked tabby. Chausies have been regarded as intelligent, active, and athletic. Chausies love to play and hate being left alone period. Loyalty is strong in Chausies and love their owners to death if the owner returns even half of the love. They are very friendly to dogs and other humans, which makes them a great addition to the house if you have dogs or kids, and have some spare time everyday to spend with them.
