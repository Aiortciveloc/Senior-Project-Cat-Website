Bengal

The Bengal Cat breed is genetically bred to look similar to jungle cats, such as leopards, tigers, and ocelots. Bengal Cats have markings, which consist of stripes, spots, rosettes, and a white belly. Also, this breed typically has a black outline around their eyes. Luckily, though, Bengal cats are much friendlier then their jungle cats look alikes, and love being with humans.
