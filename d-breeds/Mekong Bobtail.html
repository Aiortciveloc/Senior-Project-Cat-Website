Mekong Bobtail

The Mekong Bobtail cat breed originates from Thailand. In some legends of the ancient Siam (today Thailand) there were stories about beautiful cats with unusual colors and dark muzzle, dark tail and paws and blue eyes. These cats were believed to guard the palace and the treasury. Mekong Bobtail cats have a very unique tail. Each hair has its own kink and twist starting from the root. Visually, the tail is not visible, but can be felt by touch. Two identical tails of Mekong Bobtail does not happen, so the tail for the breed - it is a kind of cat identification. Their body is of rectangular format and muscular, elegant and slender. Their legs are of medium length and have oval paws. Their head has gently rounded contours, strong chin and fine oval muzzle. Their ears are broad and large, set high and slightly outwards. Eyes are large and oval is shape. The coat is short and glossy with minimal undercoat. Mekong Bobtail is the only breed of cat that behaves like a dog. These cats go very well on a leash with the owner, they like to play fetch, and are very playful. Mekong Bobtails are very devoted to their owners and don't depart from them a single step. This is a very loyal breed and, like dog. They are capable of watching for a long period of time in the eyes of human, never taking their gaze off.
