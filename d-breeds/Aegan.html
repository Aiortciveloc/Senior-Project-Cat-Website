Aegean

The Aegean cats are medium sized with semi-longhair. They usually have two or three colors on their coat, with one of the colors almost always consisting of white, which usually takes up a large chunk of their body. The Aegean breed comes from the Cycladic islands in the Aegean Sea. They are most commonly found in greece, and are considered a national treasure there. Aegeans are one of the oldest domesticated cat breeds and love being around humans. Aegean cats are affectionate, social, playful, intelligent, and love to eat fish. Aegean cats are one of the few breeds to be naturally bred and have almost no genetic diseases. These furballs are very friendly and love to give love to most people, and even dogs, which makes them a great house pet to own.
