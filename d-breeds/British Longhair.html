British Longhair

British longhairs were first bred in Great Britain. The British longhair is identical to the British Shorthair, besides fur length. Their coat is long and some say lustrous. Tails are usually thicker than other cats and have a "brush-like" feel to them. Eyes are on the lighter side of the color spectrum, and fur can come in a wide variety of colors. Living with a British Longhair is relaxing and calm, as this cat breed likes to be inactive and lay around, whether in your lap or on the furniture. They love to be indoors and never make much noise except when they're hungry of course. If you're a person that wants a quiet, loving companion around the house, think about adopting one of these fellows.
